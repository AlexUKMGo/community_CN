Who We Are

One Community,
Across the Globe

OSHIT is a community of people brought together by shared curiosity and a love for building things. We come from different cities, different backgrounds, but here we speak the same language.

From casual online hangouts to real-world meetups and community initiatives, we believe that interesting people will always find each other.

What We Do

Connect, Share,
Build Together

We gather on Telegram, Discord, and Twitter to share ideas, discuss projects, and keep each other inspired. Whether it's a quick voice chat or an in-depth thread, there's always something happening.

Offline, we've organized city cleanups, community visits, and group trips. We show up, we contribute, and we make it count.
